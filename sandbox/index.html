Learning to allocate power
in a sensing-and-communication network

A graph neural network that approximates the iterative WMMSE algorithm at a fraction of the inference cost.

Setup

Blue cars are transmitters. Each Blue serves several Yellow (sensing) and Green (communication) receivers on orthogonal channels inside its own cluster. Same-channel links from different Blue cars interfere.

Headline result

Across topologies, the trained GNN trails WMMSE on Green sum-rate by a small margin while remaining comfortably above the equal-power floor. The legend below reads higher is better.
